Local Food of Semarang

A few popular dishes. Click any image to open a bigger preview (lightbox).

Lumpia Semarang

Crispy spring rolls with bamboo shoots and savory fillings.

  • Price: Rp10k–25k
  • Where: Loenpia Mbak Lien

Tahu Gimbal

Tofu, prawn fritter, and veggies with rich peanut sauce.

  • Price: Rp15k–30k
  • Where: Around Simpang Lima

Bandeng Presto

Pressure-cooked milkfish; bones are soft and edible.

  • Price: Rp40k–90k
  • Where: Pandanaran area

Wingko Babat

Grilled coconut cake — sweet and slightly chewy.

  • Price: Rp3k–8k per piece
  • Where: JL. Pandanaran souvenir stores
